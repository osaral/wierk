wierk

/viək/

noun, neuter · plural Wierker

Luxembourgish · from Old High German werc, from Proto-Germanic *werką
cf. German Werk, English work, Dutch werk, West Frisian wurk, Icelandic verk

  1. work; achievement
  2. a work of art, literature, or craft
  3. works; factory
